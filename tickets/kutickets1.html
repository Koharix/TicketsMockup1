Bag Policy

Beginning in August 2017, fans will be allowed to bring only one clear plastic bag no larger than 12 inches by 6 inches by 12 inches or a one-gallon, clear, resealable plastic storage bag per person inside Kansas athletics events.

Fans will also be allowed a small clutch purse not to exceed 5.5 inches by 8.5 inches

Junior Jayhawks

Junior Jayhawks, the Official Kids Club for Kansas Athletics, is available to children 13 and under. Memberships last through the academic year and include a number of great benefits and exclusive access to Kansas Athletics and free admission soccer, volleyball, women's basketball, softball and baseball in addition to select football and men's basketball games. Register today.
